MindSweeper
In MindSweeper you play the part of a zombie looking for brains while avoiding hunters with shotguns. Each revealed square has clues: the numbers in red indicate how many hunters with shotguns are adjacent (including diagonally) to that square, and the numbers in white indicate how many brains are adjacent to that square. You win if you find all the brains without running into a shotgun blast! The number of brains you need to find depends on the game level, and is shown by the brains below the board.

To reveal a square that you think is safe, double-click on it. If you think you've figured out where a shotgun is, you can mark that square by clicking on it. Tips
